System Documentation

[cite_start]The AI-Powered Resume Shortlisting System is designed to automatically read, analyze, and rank resumes according to a company's specific job requirements[cite: 2, 3]. [cite_start]It extracts details from resumes (PDF/DOCX) and compares them against job descriptions to produce a ranked shortlist[cite: 4, 21].

Primary Goal

[cite_start]

To save recruiters time, increase accuracy, and make data-driven hiring decisions by removing manual bias[cite: 5, 8].

Backend (Java)

System Flow

1

Job Creation

[cite_start]

Recruiter creates a job posting with specific requirements (Skills, Exp, Keywords)[cite: 90].

2

Resume Upload

[cite_start]

Resumes are uploaded in bulk (PDF/DOCX)[cite: 92].

3

Analysis & Scoring

[cite_start]

Backend extracts text, identifies skills, and runs the matching algorithm[cite: 93, 94].

4

Dashboard Ranking

[cite_start]

Candidates are displayed on the dashboard ranked by score[cite: 95].

Scoring Breakdown (Total: 100 Points)

[cite_start]

The system uses a weighted scoring mechanism to ensure fair evaluation[cite: 22, 61].

1. Resume Format

15 Points
Clean Layout & Structure5 pts
Proper Headings3 pts
Consistent Fonts/Spacing3 pts
Professional Formatting4 pts

2. Contact Info

5 Points
Name, Phone, Email3 pts
LinkedIn/GitHub Links2 pts

4. Skills Section

15 Points
Relevant Technical Skills7 pts
Relevant Soft Skills3 pts
Categorized (Tech/Tools)3 pts
No Spam Skills2 pts

5. Experience

20 Points
Job Titles + Company5 pts
Proper Timeline3 pts
Quantifiable Achievements (Numbers)5 pts
Job Relevance2 pts
[cite_start]

*Remaining 45 points distributed across Projects (15), Education (10), Certs (5), Summary (10), etc [cite: 23-61].
