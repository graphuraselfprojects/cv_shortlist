Frequently Asked Questions

How many resumes can I upload?

The system processes resumes one by one from your bulk upload. For the demo version, we recommend uploading up to 20 files at a time for optimal speed.

Is the parsing accurate?

We target ≥85% parsing accuracy using advanced NLP libraries like Apache OpenNLP and PDFBox.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use JWT authentication and do not share candidate data with third parties.
